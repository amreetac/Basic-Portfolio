About Me

Hi. My name is Amreeta Choudhury. I have a Masters degree of Statistics and Biostatistics from Rutgers University. Over the past few years, I have had an interesting history as a Recruiter, Adjunct Professor of NJIT, a Data Science Consultant and this past year, I worked in Wall Street as a Statistical analyst for Full Beauty Brands, a corporation owning online and retail stores Lane Bryant, King Size, Brylane Home, Swimsuits for all. Throughout my career, the one thing that always stood out to me was the website and programming component of my jobs. I would train many consultants in becoming skilled in the up and coming web languguages and I thought, why not me?

I love designing websites. In my current role, I am instrumental in overseeing good landing pages for Sollers Institute's Data Science programs. As a kid, I would play around code in Myspace and GUI programs such as Unbounce, a nice platform for making websites. Now I get to follow my dream and create them myself!

For fun, I love traveling! My goal is to become the best developer I can be but also to visit each continent at least once as well! My favorite vacations have been in Alaska and Greece. Pictures to come soon! Meanwhile, check out my profile on Github or feel free to send me an email anytime!
